10 Coolest Things of the Week

Keith Haring retrospect skateboard by The Skateroom

From the guy that did the kids health adverts comes six brilliant skateboard designs. The Skateroom's latest release of Keith Haring skateboard decks. £825. At Mr Porter. mrporter.com

Rémy Martin x The Connaught x Reiko Kaneko

If you are a cognac fan then you will love the collaboration between The Connaught Hotel, Remymartin and ceramicist Reitko Kaneko. For london craft week they have made a black bone china cup for this gorgeous cognac to be consumed in. It wil be served throughout autumn so be ready! the-connaught.co.uk

Lego Lamborghini Sián

If you like supercars and childrens toys then you will love the lego lamborghini Sián. 3,696-piece kit. It comes with scissor doors, a v12 engine, front and rear suspension and a working gear box. This means you can have hours of fun. all without remortgaging your house. £349.99. lego.com

Monopoly Bob Ross Edition

The most stressful board game in the world has just had a tranquil makeover, with Monopoly's special Bob Ross edition of the game. The man renowned for his calming painting and voice, Bob Ross, is making an appearance with the most stressful game ever. £30.32. hasbropulse.com

Fender’s Player Series

Fender’s newly launched Player Series not only looks great, but these stunning new models have upgraded features such as vintage-inspired pickups. My top pick is the firemist gold Mustang: a classic style upgraded with great electronics and performance related features. £549. fender.com
